About Me

Brittanie Boyko is a web developer in training. She has three years of experience in the tech industry with a focus on quality assurance and mobile development in both independent and team-oriented environments. Brittanie also eats food, pets dogs, and writes bios about herself in the third person as an attempt to sound more impressive. She'd love to talk more with you. Drop her a line via the contact page!
